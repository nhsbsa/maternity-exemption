Register to apply for a maternity exemption certificate What you'll need

Use this service to register to apply for maternity exemption certificate .

You must be a Midwife, GP or Health Visitor who is registered with the NMC or GMC to create an account.

To register we will ask information about your:

  • name
  • date of birth (if you are NMC registered)
  • NMC/GMC number
  • NHS email address

Registration takes around 5 minutes.
