Use this service to apply for maternity exemption for new mother and expectant mother.

Maternity exemptions certificate entitles the mother for free prescriptions

  • while pregnant
  • 12 months after the birth of the child

You will need the mother's:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • due or birth date of the child
  • email or postal address

Application takes around 5 minutes.
