Register as a GP to issue maternity exemption certificates

If you are a GP registered with the General Medical Council (GMC) you can still register to use this service.

To get registered, email nhsbsa.healthcosts@nhs.net and include your:

  • name
  • GMC reference number
  • NHSmail address

    • Once we’ve received this information, we’ll email you to confirm you are registered. You will then be able to use the online service.
